# Neon Apex Rush 3D

A pseudo-3D arcade kart racer with a technical desert circuit, 7 AI opponents, and obstacle-heavy sectors.

## Controls

- `←` / `→`: steer and drift through corners
- `Space` or **Turbo** button: speed boost

## Gameplay loop

- Race against 7 AI karts with dynamic lane decisions.
- Survive realistic turn sequences and frequent barrier/cone hazards.
- Hit turbo on exits, then recover during cooldown while chasing your best score.
